Contact the UK Emissions Trading Scheme reporting helpdesk

If you need help accessing or using the UK Emissions Trading Scheme reporting service, please contact your regulator.

  • Environment Agency (EA): ethelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
  • Scottish Environment Protection Agency (SEPA): emission.trading@sepa.org.uk
  • Northern Ireland Environment Agency (NIEA): emissions.trading@daera-ni.gov.uk
  • Natural Resources Wales (NRW): GHGHelp@naturalresourceswales.gov.uk
  • Offshore Petroleum Regulator for Environment & Decommissioning (OPRED): opred@energysecurity.gov.uk
  • EA (for aviation): etaviationhelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
